How to play

The game secretly chooses five blocks and stacks them.

  • Tap blocks to add/remove them.
  • Tap slots to swap their positions.

EASY MODE:

  • Perfect blocks lock in place.
  • Duds are grayed out so you don't pick them again.
  • Look for the Green/Yellow/Gray dots in the log.

NOT SO EASY MODE:

  • No locking. No graying out.
  • Use the text clues at the bottom to solve it!
