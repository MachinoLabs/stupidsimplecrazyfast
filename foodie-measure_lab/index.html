Practical Kitchen Tips

Short reminders that actually help when you’re in the middle of cooking.

  • ⚖️ For baking, weighing dry ingredients (especially flour and sugar) is more reliable than scooping with cups.
  • 🥄 When using cups, lightly spoon dry ingredients in and level the top with a straight edge instead of packing them down.
  • 🔥 Always give the oven time to fully preheat and use the center rack unless a recipe clearly says otherwise.
  • ⏱️ Scaled recipes may need a little more or less time. Start checking doneness a bit earlier instead of relying on the clock alone.
